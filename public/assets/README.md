# Replace these placeholder files with your own media

## Images
Place your photos in these folders:

```
public/assets/images/
├── hero/          → parents.jpg (main hero photo)
├── wedding/       → wedding photos
├── childhood/     → baby & childhood photos
├── trips/         → family trip photos
├── festivals/     → festival celebration photos
├── recent/        → recent family photos
├── then-vs-now/   → wedding photo (then) & current photo (now)
└── family/        → individual photos for family tree
```

Then update paths in `src/data/images.js`

## Audio
Place your audio files here:

```
public/assets/audio/
├── background-music.mp3   → romantic instrumental background music
└── voice-message.mp3      → voice message from Shreya & Swanand
```

Then update paths in `src/data/config.js` if needed.
